    ABOUT HACK-A-THON

    ABOUT HACK-A-THON

    HackaversuMM, a National Level Hackathon, is going to be organized by Maharishi Markandeshwar (Deemed to be University), Mullana, Ambala (Haryana), India during the first week of September. HackaversuMM is an event that brings together Developers, Hackers and Technology enthusiasts to develop innovative solutions to the challenges in society, industry and the environment. It's a fantastic opportunity for developing indigenous tech solutions that are innovative and can be turned into a functional prototype with the help of industry mentors.

    Themes

    Themes

    Infrastructure

    Infrastructure being the underlying structure of a country and its economy has a huge potential of scaling it. Dive into cracking various infrastructure problems such as regulatory risks, environmental impact, land acquisition and many more.

    Travel and Tourism

    With the world opening up there is a need to travel uphills for making a mark in travel and tourism. Decode travel and tourism in a way that it boosts efficiency and effectiveness for the travel and tourism industry by having a crack at problems such as access and connectivity, sustainable and sufficient accommodation and proper amenities, tourists security and many more.

    Smart Education

    With the world drifting towards technology, why not make room for smart education. So indulge yourself in tracking innovative solutions for problems such as Privacy and security of students, online work tracking, virtual class engagement and many more.

    Smart Agriculture

    Farming is all about getting in touch with earth around us so, enhancing it with technology, why not? Go ahead and thrive for innovative solutions for Farming with the art and science that manages precise cultivation, soil monitoring, crop sowing, etc.

    Health Care

    The art of medicine consists of amusing the patients while nature cures the disease. Go ahead and innovate health care system problems related to patients data management, healthcare cost, evidence based emergency treatments and many more.

    Event Timelin

    Registration (Idea Submission)

    All the teams are required to get themselves registered, choose a theme from the given themes and submit their PPT in the given format by 10th August, 2022.

    18th July
    10th August

    Top Teams Shortlisted

    All the registered teams will be evaluated by the experts on the basis of Novelty, Appropriateness, Feasibility, Relevance, Impact and Scalability. The top teams will get shortlisted and notified by 17th of August.

    18th July
    10th August

    Finale Kickstart

    Teams that have advanced from Round 1 will compete in the final round, which will be of 30 hours in which teams will have to create the prototype as well as to present their solutions to a panel of experts. This round will take place in physical manner where all the qualified teams have to report at MMDU campus.

    10th August
